Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and the smallest planet in our solar system. It completes one orbit around the Sun in just 88 Earth days.

  • Distance from Sun: 57.9 million km
  • Diameter: 4,879 km
  • Gravity: 3.7 m/s²
  • Length of Year: 88 Earth days
  • Number of Moons: 0

Interesting Facts

Closest Planet

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun in our solar system.

Extreme Temperatures

Temperatures range from -180°C at night to 430°C during the day.

No Atmosphere

Mercury has almost no atmosphere, which is why temperatures vary greatly.
